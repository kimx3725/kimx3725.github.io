Bio

I am currently a PhD Candidate at the University of Minnesota Twin Cities (UMN), in the Department of Ecology, Evolution, and Behavior, under the supervision of Allison Shaw and John Fieberg. Previously, I completed a BA in Statistics at the University of Minnesota, Morris advised by Jon Andersen, where I worked on regression methods in Biostatistics.

My research interests are in applied statistics in movement ecology. I am currently interested in quantifying the effects of hidden factors that influence animal movement (e.g., disease, cognition, traits).
